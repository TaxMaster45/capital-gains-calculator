Standard Deduction 2025: How Much Can You Save?

Published: December 16, 2025 | Category: Tax Guide

What is it? The Standard Deduction is a specific dollar amount that reduces the income you’re taxed on. It requires no receipts or forms—you just claim it!

New Limits for 2025 (Filing in 2026)

Due to inflation, the IRS has increased the standard deduction amounts. Here are the new numbers:

Filing Status 2025 Deduction Amount
Single $15,000
Married Filing Jointly $30,000
Head of Household $22,500

Standard vs. Itemized Deduction

You have a choice: take the Standard Deduction OR Itemize your deductions.

  • Standard: Easy, fast, no proof needed. Best if your total expenses are low.
  • Itemized: Requires listing expenses like mortgage interest, medical bills, and charity. Only do this if your expenses are higher than the standard amount shown above.

For most people, the Standard Deduction is the better choice because it saves time and offers a large tax break automatically.
