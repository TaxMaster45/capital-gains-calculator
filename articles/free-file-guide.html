How to File Your US Taxes for Free in 2025: A Complete Guide

Every year, millions of Americans pay hundreds of dollars to file their taxes, not realizing they could have done it for absolutely free. Whether you are a student, a freelancer, or a W-2 employee, there are several IRS-approved ways to file without spending a dime.

Quick Summary: If your Adjusted Gross Income (AGI) is $79,000 or less, you qualify for the IRS Free File program, which gives you access to top-tier software like TaxAct and FreeTaxUSA for free.

1. The IRS Free File Program

The IRS partners with several private tax software companies to provide free federal tax preparation and e-filing. For 2025, the income limit is $79,000. This is the most secure way to file if you meet the income requirements.

2. Cash App Taxes (Formerly Credit Karma Taxes)

If you don't qualify for IRS Free File due to higher income, Cash App Taxes is one of the only truly free options that supports federal AND state returns for free, even for complex situations like stock sales or rental income.
