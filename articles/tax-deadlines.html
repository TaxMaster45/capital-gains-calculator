2025 Tax Calendar: Key Dates and Deadlines You Must Know

Missing an IRS deadline can result in expensive penalties and interest charges. To help you stay on track, we have compiled the most important federal tax dates for the 2025 calendar year.

Individual Filing Deadlines

JAN 27
Tax Season Opens: The IRS typically begins accepting and processing 2024 tax returns in late January.
APR 15
Tax Day: This is the final deadline to file your individual tax return or request a 6-month extension.
OCT 15
Extension Deadline: If you requested an extension, this is the final day to file your 2024 tax return.
Pro Tip: Don't wait until April 15! Filing early is the best way to protect yourself from tax-related identity theft and to get your refund faster.

Estimated Tax Payments (for Freelancers/Gig Workers)

If you are self-employed, you must pay taxes quarterly. Here are the 2025 due dates:

  • Q1 Payment: April 15, 2025
  • Q2 Payment: June 16, 2025
  • Q3 Payment: September 15, 2025
  • Q4 Payment: January 15, 2026
