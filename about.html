About Us

Welcome to TexMaster, your go-to platform for simplified tax estimation and financial planning tools.

Who We Are

We are a team of tech enthusiasts and finance researchers dedicated to making complex tax calculations easy for everyone. We understand that filing taxes or estimating deductions can be confusing, especially for beginners and freelancers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide fast, free, and user-friendly tools that help you estimate your tax liabilities specifically for the US tax system. We aim to save your time by automating the complex math behind tax brackets and deductions.

Data Sources & Accuracy

Our calculators are built based on publicly available tax tables and guidelines from the IRS (Internal Revenue Service). However, we always recommend consulting a certified tax professional for official filing.
