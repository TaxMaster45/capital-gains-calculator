TexMaster follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, and referring/exit pages.

Like any other website, TexMaster uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.
